My life

I’ve been drawing for as long as I remember..  Back in 2010,I started watching anime and reading manga,I was so inspired by the unique art styles in anime and manga..I think thats around when I started taking art a little bit more seriously, drawing everyday and drawing what I loved. I have improved a lot, but I'm still nowhere where I want to be.
